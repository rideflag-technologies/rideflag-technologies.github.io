Total qualifying Carpool

Rules
Updated November 19th, 2021

Complete a minimum of 20 trips between the first and last day of the month to receive a $100 Amazon e-gift card. Drivers must use HOV mode on their Express Lane pass to receive toll-free access. A maximum of 4 trips per day will count towards your total qualifying carpool requirement. All trips must be completed on the l-15 Express Lanes.
